FAQ

Before You Reach Out

How long does a project take?

It depends on scope - a quick fix or landing page can take a few days, while a full school ERP or business platform typically takes 4-10 weeks. We give you a realistic timeline after the discovery conversation.

Can you improve an existing system?

Yes. We regularly take over, audit, and extend systems built by other developers - adding features, fixing bugs, or migrating them to more reliable infrastructure.

Do you provide hosting?

Yes. We offer website and application hosting, ongoing maintenance, and technical support plans so your system stays online and up to date after launch.
