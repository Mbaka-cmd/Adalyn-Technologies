Why Students Work With Us

We've Seen What Gets Marked Down - and Fixed It

Most final year projects lose marks for the same reasons: no proper documentation, a system that only runs on the student's own laptop, a database design that doesn't hold up under questioning, or a presentation that falls apart under a simple "what if" from the panel.

FAQ

Questions Students Ask Us

Will this count as my own work?

We work with you as technical support - you remain the author and need to understand your project well enough to defend it. We recommend checking your institution's policy on external technical help before engaging us.

How close to my deadline can I come to you?

The earlier the better, but we regularly help students under tight deadlines. Message us with your deadline and we'll tell you honestly whether it's realistic.

Can you just review my existing project?

Yes. We can audit an existing project, fix bugs, improve documentation, or handle deployment without rebuilding it from scratch.

Do you help with the oral defense too?

Yes, we walk through likely panel questions with you and make sure you can explain every technical decision in your system.
